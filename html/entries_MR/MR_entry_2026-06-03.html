Floor Controller PCB solder test and CAD model development

My logbook cover page

STM32 Nucleo Shield Boards

I communicated with the tool room technician, Sam Butella, to sign out materials to make 4 Nucleo shields which are used for floor controllers. However, there was no stencil for solder paste application. 1 board was assembled to see the result of baking a PCB without a stencil. The soldering quality is rather poor, however, the PCB can be touched up manually with a soldering iron to fix this.

Additionally, some components were missing from the FC PCB parts kit. I reached out to the tool room technician to find substitute components.
