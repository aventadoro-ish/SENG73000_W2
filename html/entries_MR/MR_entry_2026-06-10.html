Nucleo Shield Soldering

I started soldering 3 remaining Nucleo Shields by hand, as the results from the test board baked w/o a stencil were quite poor. It was estimated that soldering by hand would take less time than baking and touching up boards manually afterwards.

Soldering went smoothly, although 0201 capacitors C3 and C4 were time-consuming to solder. Additionally, 1x C3 cap is missing, as all the spares were lost during the soldering process. For the time being, it was deemed unnecessary, as the 15pF C3 simply adds capacitance to the Rx line. Further testing is needed.

Missing PCAN adapter found

Sam Butella, the Tool Room Technician, found the missing PCAN adapter, so now the RPi does not need to use MCP module (SPI-CAN adapter).
