CAD Model of the Elevator

I drafted an initial CAD model of the elevator. The construction uses 2020 T- and V-slot aluminum extrusions. This way was chosen to make the platfrom easy to assemble in a short amount of time. Linear motion of the elevator will be supported by a roller gantry assembly, similar to those used in construction of 3D printers. 2 gantries will provide mounting points for elevator car and its counterweight. The gantries are mounted to 2 V-slot extrusions at the center of the assembly. Additional 4 corner T-slot extrusions will provide means of mounting electronics and decorative elements to the assembly.
