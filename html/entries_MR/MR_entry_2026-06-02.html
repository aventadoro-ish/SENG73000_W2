Designing elevator motor mount subassembly

Designed parts to mount main elevator motor and a pulley system to route cable holding elevator car and counterwight.

The subassembly consists of 2 plates that are attached to the top 2020 extrusions. Right plate mounts a stepper motor. Left plate has mounting holes for a drag chain mount which will be used to run the CAN bus cable to the elevator car. The plates use 5mm ID, 11mm OD, 4mm thickness ball bearings to provide low-friction rotation for the pulleys and offload motor shaft.

The pulley system consists of 3 wheels. One pulley is 50mm in diameter and is attached to the stepper motor shaft. 2 additional 25mm-diamter pulleys are used to route the cable such that it runs vertically to the car and counterwieght without collision with other parts for the platform.
